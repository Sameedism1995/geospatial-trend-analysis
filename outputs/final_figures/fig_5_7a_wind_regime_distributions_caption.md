## Figure 5.7a — Wind-regime participation by distance bands

Indicators are summarized with **within-variable rank percentiles** computed on the plotted observation slice (MEI via `maritime_pressure_index`, week-aligned vessel density, atmospheric-transfer index, and **NO₂ excess** defined as observation-level NO₂ minus the weekly pooled median). Distance annuli tighten to coastal rings 0–30 km referenced to `nearest_port`. Violins use width-normalised densities with quartile enclosures for medians.
