## Figure 5.7b — Directional regime comparison

Three outcome panels summarise **means ± SE** for vessel density (`vessel_density_t`), Maritime Exposure Index (`maritime_pressure_index`), and weekly NO₂. Within each subplot, clustered bars contrast shoreward versus non-shoreward classifications at each archival `nearest_port` assignment (Turku, Mariehamn); numerals atop bars give means, while **n=** captions record analytic sample sizes.
